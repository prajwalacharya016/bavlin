Proposal

Partnership structure and engagement.

This outline is not legally binding—it is a guideline. Any partnership would be subject to formal, legally binding agreements.

1. Ownership structure

Bavlin would hold a majority stake; the entrepreneur(s) would be provided with equity. No financial investment is required from the entrepreneur(s)—Bavlin provides all capital.

2. Our role

We are an active partner: daily operations and management, investing and capital deployment, and governance and strategy—not hands-off.

3. No investment required

Entrepreneurs contribute commitment, expertise, and leadership—not capital. Equity is allocated to reflect that and align incentives.

For full details, see the full proposal. For inquiries, get in touch through the contact section below.
